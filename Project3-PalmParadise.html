PALM PARADISE FARMSTAY

LOCATION - Chon gaon, Badlapur, Maharashtra

PROJECT TYPE - Architectural Design

PROJECT STATUS - ONGOING

AREA - 4 Acre

The farmstay is located in Chon gaon, 65km away from Mumbai. It is owned by a well known doctor in Badlapur and was used as personal farm house for the family. Later it was decided to convert it into a farm stay. It had beautiful context with tall palm trees, a lawn area in middle and lush green everywhere. Dr.Samel being a fond of art & architecture, decided to design and plan the farm stay by herself. She had already planned and started execution of cottages and 2 tents. As located in the outskirts and lack of skilled labours the 2 tents planned by Dr.Samel lost in scale and were not appropriately fitting to the pre-fabricated MS frame structure. She approached us with this problem and asked to suggest a solution for it.

After visiting the site and considering the already done construction, we suggested Dr.Samel to shift the tents to a different location in the farm and plan a new cottage at the location of tents. The Cottage 2 (In Cover Page) has been planned by us considering the pre-fabricated frame structure. Also we suggested few changes to the Cottage 1 with Grill design, window chajjas. We also planned the new location for the tents.

The tree house was the dream project and prime focus of Dr.Samel as it was already planned by her. It was designed to look like a natural rock structure. A live mahua tree passed vertically through the tree house and all spaces were planned alongside the tree. It had a earth mound spa room and a guest room with interior in rocks at the ground floor while two guest rooms at the first floor. The whole structure was Mild Steel Fabricated with use of GRC(Glass Reinforced Concrete) for walls to look like rocks. The tree house was already in execution phase when we visited the site. The interior planning, materials and suggestions for the services were provided by us.

We are also designing, a communtiy hall with capacity of 75people and a souvenir shop. Landscape planning is also taken into consideration. We have also designed furniture from scratch material with use of resin. Also many more artistic details from waste material are been designed to make farm stay sustainable.
