OFFICE SPACE FOR FACTORY

LOCATION - Wardha, Maharashtra

PROJECT TYPE - Interior Design

PROJECT STATUS - PROPOSED & FINALIZED

AREA - 1150 Sq.Ft

This interior project is of an office space for factory owner in Wardha. Being a workplace we have tried keeping the spaces as lively as possible through the colour schemes using vibrant color sparks in an overall white and brown pallete. The interior has been focused on a lavish workspace as per the clients desire.
